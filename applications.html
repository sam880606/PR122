Application Areas

Plastics

Pigment Red 122 is widely used in plastics (PVC, PP, PE, etc.) for its vibrant color, UV resistance, and thermal stability. It enhances product aesthetics and maintains color integrity during high-temperature processing and outdoor use.

Coatings and Inks

In industrial and automotive coatings, Pigment Red 122 offers exceptional weather and chemical resistance, ensuring long-lasting color performance in harsh environments. Its high color strength allows for efficient pigment usage.

Textiles

Pigment Red 122 is suitable for fiber and dyeing processes, providing wash and wear resistance and excellent color fastness to prevent fading and bleeding.

Other Applications

Pigment Red 122 is also used in high-performance and composite materials to enhance color performance and improve material durability.

Pigment Red 122 Technical Highlights and Usage Scenarios

Key explanations of when to use Pigment Red 122 and its technical characteristics:

High-Saturation Tinting

Advantages: Achieves ideal hues quickly, improving tinting efficiency due to its high saturation.

Application: Suitable for basic tinting in coatings, inks, and plastics.

High Coloring Power and Acid-Alkali Resistance

Advantages: Provides vivid and uniform color at low levels with stability in harsh chemical environments.

Application: Ideal for industrial coatings needing high chemical stability.

Transparency, Dispersibility, and High Coloring Power

Advantages: Excellent transparency and dispersibility ensure consistent color performance.

Application: Outstanding in inks, spray paints, and special crafts requiring delicate color effects.

High-Temperature Resistance Tinting at Low Concentrations

Advantages: Maintains excellent high-temperature resistance even at low concentrations.

Application: Suitable for systems needing color stability in high-temperature operations.

Excellent Performance in Outdoor Applications

Advantages: Exhibits excellent light and weather resistance, maintaining color stability long-term.

Application: Ideal for building exteriors and outdoor coatings needing long-term anti-aging properties.
